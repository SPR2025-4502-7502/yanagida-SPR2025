Maps of Cayli's top 10 favorite mountains!

Here are maps of my top 10 favorite mountains across the world.

These mountains include Stanage Edge in England's Peak District, Wellhorn in Switzerland's Interlaken and Secëda in the Italian Dolomites.

These mountains include both the North and South American mountains in the first list. The list includes Rainier in Washington State and Oberlin in Montana within the United States, as well as Machu Picchu in Peru and the Cerro Torre in Chile.

These mountains include those that are found on islands and small island nations, excluding England. The Ko'olau Range is found on the Hawaiian island of O'ahu, where my parents grew up. Vestrahorn is on Iceland, and Mýlingur is located on the island of Streymoy, which is part of the Faroe Islands.
