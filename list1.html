Cayli's top 10 favorite mountains

Rainier

Washington State, United States, 46.8523° N, 121.7603° W

Growing up in Washington State, I grew up seeing this mountain almost every day. Mount Rainier is the largest mountain in the contiguous United States, and ranks first due to its iconic structure and personal meaning.
