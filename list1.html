Cayli's top 10 favorite mountains ranked

Here’s a list of my top 10 favorite mountains across the world.

  1. Rainier

    2. Washington State, United States 46.8523° N, 121.7603° W

    Growing up in Washington State, I grew up seeing this mountain almost every day. Mount Rainier is the largest mountain in the contiguous United States, and ranks first due to its iconic structure and personal meaning.

  2. Cerro Torre

    3. El Chaten, Patagonia, Argentina/Chile 49.2931° S, 73.0979° W

    Although I’ve never been to Patagonia, it has always been a lifelong dream. This mountain captured my attention because of its unusual shape and isolated location.

  3. Vestrahorn

    4. Höfn, Iceland 64.2733° N, 15.0086° W

    I’ve been to Iceland twice, but I’ve never seen this mountain. On the eastern part of the island, it is on the opposite side from Reykjavik, the capital city.

  4. Ko'olau Range

    5. Kaneohe, O’ahu, Hawai’i, United States 21°27′0″N 157°54′0″W﻿ / ﻿21.45000°N 157.90000°W

    My parents are from Hawai’i, so I grew up traveling through the mountains in order to get to my dad’s hometown of Kaneohe.

  5. Seceda

    6. The Dolomites, Italy 46.5981° N, 11.7242° E

    The Dolomites boast some incredible peaks, but this one is on my bucket list.

  6. Stanage Edge

    7. Peak District National Park, Derbyshire, England, United Kingdom 53.3473° N, 1.6333° W

    As what is quite possibly my favorite place on Earth, Stanage Edge ranks sixth because it’s technically not a mountain — but who cares?

  7. Machu Picchu

    8. Aguas Calientes, Peru 13.1632° S, 72.5453° W

    I visited Machu Picchu when I was 15 years old. The memory will stick with me forever.

  8. Mylingur

    9. Streymoy Island, Faroe Islands 62.3017° N, 7.2128° W

    Another cliff that’s technically not a mountain, but is just too good to leave out.

  9. Oberlin

    10. Glacier National Park, Montana, United States 48° 42' 16'' N, 113° 44' 12'' W

    Montana has to be one of the most beautiful places on the planet. Bucket list material!!!

  10. Wellhorn

    11. Bernese Oberland, Switzerland 46.6557° N, 8.1420° E

    Ranking last, Wellhorn’s stunning and unique structure makes it a top 10 favorite.
