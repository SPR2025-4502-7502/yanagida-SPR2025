Star Wars Movies from Best to Worst

Here’s a ranked list of the Skywalker Saga Star Wars movies from best to worst.

Episode V — The Empire Strikes Back

One of the most iconic movies of all time. “Luke, I am your father.” “I love you. I know.” Like, come on.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode III — Revenge of the Sith

Hear me out. I know it’s a prequel movie, and that’s the reason why it’s not first. But it’s so good!

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode IV — A New Hope

The movie that started it all. But it can only be third when you take into account the CGI and story pacing.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode II — Attack of the Clones

The prequels are better than people give them credit for being. That’s all I have to say here.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode I — The Phantom Menace

“Duel of the Fates” single handedly brings this movie up to fifth place.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode VI — Return of the Jedi

While iconic, there were parts that were so random it almost felt like there was no plot. It’s still better than the sequels, though.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode VII — The Force Awakens

It was basically just “A New Hope” with different characters.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode IX — The Rise of Skywalker

An anti-climatic ending to one of the best movie series of all time.

Photo courtesy of Amazon

Episode VII — The Last Jedi

We don’t even need to talk about this one.

Photo courtesy of Amazon
